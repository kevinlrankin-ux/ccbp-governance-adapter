# Commercial Use

Commercial Use requires a separate written agreement ("Commercial Addendum").

## Summary
- Community/CLP-Compliant use: permitted under LICENSE
- Commercial use: requires Commercial Addendum

## Commercial cap concept
Commercial terms may include an IP participation / royalty arrangement capped at 5%,
as negotiated and stated in the Commercial Addendum.

To request commercial terms: open an issue labeled `commercial` or contact the repository owner.
